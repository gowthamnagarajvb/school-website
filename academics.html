Curriculum & Philosophy

Discovery Montessori preschools adhere to the Montessori educational philosophy developed by Dr. Maria Montessori. This approach emphasizes child-centered, hands-on learning experiences, allowing children to explore and learn at their own pace within a prepared environment. Our curriculum supports holistic child development through Practical Life, Sensorial, Language Arts, Mathematics, Science, and Outdoor Education.

Core Curriculum

  • Practical Life: Daily living skills, independence.
  • Sensorial: Refining senses and discrimination.
  • Language: Phonetics, reading, and writing.
  • Mathematics: Numbers, operations, geometry.
  • Cultural Studies: Geography, science, art, music.

World-Class Facilities

  • Montessori Materials: Hands-on, self-correcting.
  • Child-Sized Furniture: Independent access.
  • Activity Rooms: Art, Music, Practical Life areas.
  • Outdoor: Playground, Gardening, Sand play.
  • Wellness: Nap room, Quiet corners.

Safety & Security

  • CCTV Surveillance: Classrooms & common areas.
  • Secure Entry: ID verification & visitor logs.
  • Staff Checks: Police verification for all staff.
  • Transport: GPS tracking & trained attendants.
  • Health: First aid & emergency protocols.
