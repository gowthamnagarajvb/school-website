Document Details

Admission Form

You can download the admission form from our website. Fill it, scan it and submit it in just a few clicks.

Residence Proof

You can submit any of these – Electricity Bill, Driver’s License, Aadhar Card or PAN Card.

2 Passport Sizes Photos

Please submit two recent passport size photos of your child.

School Leaving Certificate

If your child has moved from another school, then please submit the school leaving certificate!

Immunisation Record

Please submit your child’s medical history and the record of vaccination and blood type for medical emergencies.

FAQ’s

What is the admission procedure?

The admission process begins with booking an online counselling session with an expert followed by collecting the school prospectus and brochure, then submitting the admission form. Finally, enrol your child for the Skill Assessment Session to evaluate their individual needs.

What are the school timings?

Please contact the school administration for the specific timings for each grade.

What is the fee structure?

Our fee structure is designed to be transparent. Please visit the campus or contact us for the detailed fee schedule.

Which curriculum or board do you follow?

We follow both CBSE and ICSE curriculums, providing a comprehensive educational experience.

Which documents are required for seeking admission?

Required documents include Admission Form, Residence Proof, Passport Size Photos, School Leaving Certificate, and Immunisation Record.

Are transport facilities available?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transport facilities covering major routes.
